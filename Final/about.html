Our Story

Redefining Winter Essentials

THE ANNEX

Founded in 2020, THE ANNEX represents a commitment to timeless design and uncompromising quality. We believe that true style transcends trends, focusing instead on craftsmanship and sustainability.

Each piece in our collection is carefully designed to merge functionality with minimalist aesthetics, creating wardrobe essentials that last beyond seasons.

Our approach is simple: premium materials, ethical production, and designs that speak for themselves. We create pieces for those who value quality over quantity.

Our Values

Quality

Premium materials sourced from trusted suppliers. Every piece is crafted to last, ensuring longevity and timeless appeal.

Sustainability

Ethical production practices and eco-conscious materials. We believe in fashion that respects both people and planet.

Design

Minimalist aesthetics that never go out of style. Clean lines, thoughtful details, and versatile pieces for modern living.

Craftsmanship

Attention to detail in every stitch. Our garments are produced in small batches by skilled artisans in Portugal.
